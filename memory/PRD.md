# Valentine's Day Website for Harika - PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Create a Valentine's Day website for Harika from Umesh with:
- Glassmorphism login form with dark abstract wallpaper background
- Password: "Harika@gandham"
- Valentine's week days (Rose Day to Valentine's Day) with content
- Heartfelt apology/missing letter
- Telugu love songs playlist (Nuvuntey Chaley, Ayyayo) embedded from YouTube
- Click particle animation with multiple colors
- Photos of Umesh and Harika
- Advanced UI/UX

## User Personas

- **Creator**: Umesh - expressing love and apology
